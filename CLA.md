# Contributor License Agreement (CLA) / 贡献者许可协议

[English](#english) · [中文](#中文)

> **Why this exists:** Genesis.ai is dual-licensed (AGPL-3.0 + commercial).
> To keep offering a commercial license, the project maintainer must hold (or
> be licensed to sublicense) the rights to **all** contributed code. This CLA
> grants those rights while letting you keep ownership of your contribution.
>
> Signing is automated: the **CLA Assistant** bot will comment on your first
> pull request and record your agreement when you reply. You only sign once.

7. **As is.** Unless required by law, You provide Your Contribution "AS IS"
   without warranties of any kind.
