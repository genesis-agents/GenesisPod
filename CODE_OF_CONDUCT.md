# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
Genesis.ai community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant] as its
Code of Conduct. Please read the full text for the detailed examples of
positive behavior and of unacceptable behavior, as well as the enforcement
guidelines:

- Full text: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, give and accept constructive
feedback gracefully, and prioritize what is best for the overall community.
Harassment and exclusionary behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other project channels — and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the project maintainers through the repository's
**Security → Report a vulnerability** channel or another private channel agreed
with the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of
the reporter of any incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may
face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the
project's leadership.
