# Research Strategies

> System-level research strategy rules. Analogous to Karpathy's program.md.
> These rules are loaded at startup and applied to guide iterative research.
> Updated automatically as the system accumulates experience.

## Search Rules

- GLOBAL_TOPIC: 至少 30% 搜索轮次使用英文 query
- ACADEMIC_TOPIC: 优先使用 academic 类型搜索步骤，添加 "research paper study" 关键词
- CURRENT_EVENTS: 添加时间限定词 (近 7 天/30 天)
- MARKET_DATA: 搜索 query 中加入 "statista", "report", "data", "statistics" 等关键词

## Demo Rules

- AUDIENCE_RESEARCH: Demo 必须包含 ≥3 个细分画像卡片
- MARKET_RESEARCH: Demo 必须有可交互的时间趋势图
- TECHNOLOGY_RESEARCH: Demo 需要技术对比矩阵或架构图
- PRODUCT_RESEARCH: Demo 需要可操作的产品原型 (至少3个视图)
- STRATEGY_RESEARCH: Demo 需要决策模拟器 (参数可调，结果可视化)

## Agent Config Rules

- CONTROVERSIAL_TOPIC: Analyst creativity 提高到 high，鼓励质疑
- TECHNICAL_TOPIC: Researcher C 视角从"社会影响"换为"技术架构深度"
